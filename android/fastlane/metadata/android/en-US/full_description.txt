I built Mars Launcher because I couldn't stand my own phone anymore.
Too many icons. Too much color. Too many things designed to pull me back in. Mars Launcher is my attempt to fix that — a minimal, text-based home screen that turns your phone into a tool instead of a trap.

<b>🚀 CORE NAVIGATION</b>
Swipe up, type, done. Mars Launcher opens your app the moment the result is unique — no extra tap needed. Seven shortcuts for your daily drivers, swipe left or right for instant custom shortcuts. Long-press the empty space for settings.

<b>HIDDEN TOOLS</b>
A few things I added quietly, for the people who actually explore. Long-press the clock for a quick alarm, the date for a minimal to-do list, the weather widget for local sunrise and sunset times.

<b>LOOKS &amp; FEEL</b>
Double-tap the empty space to switch between dark and light mode. Pure black background, easy on the eyes and the battery. Colors, fonts and UI adjustable to your taste.

<b>WHAT MARS WILL NEVER HAVE</b>
No ads. No tracking. No subscription. No bullshit.

Mars Launcher is a small indie project — open-source, free forever, and built with the belief that your attention is worth protecting.

<i>PS: Mars stands for Minimalistic and Really Simple :)</i>
