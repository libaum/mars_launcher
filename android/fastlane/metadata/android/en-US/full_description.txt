I built Mars because I couldn't stand my own phone anymore.<br><br>

Too many icons. Too much color. Too many things designed to pull me back in. Mars Launcher is my attempt to fix that — a minimal, text-based home screen that turns your phone into a tool instead of a trap.<br><br>

It started as a personal project. Now maybe it's yours too.<br><br>

<b>🚀 CORE NAVIGATION</b><br><br>
- <b>Instant Launch</b> — Swipe up, start typing. The moment your search is unique, the app opens. No tap needed.<br>
- <b>7 Text Shortcuts</b> — Tap to launch, long-press to assign.<br>
- <b>Swipe Left/Right</b> — Jump to custom shortcuts instantly.<br>
- <b>Settings</b> — Long-press the empty space.<br><br>

<b>HIDDEN TOOLS</b><br><br>
- Clock → long-press for a quick alarm<br>
- Date → long-press for a minimal to-do list<br>
- Weather → long-press for local sunrise &amp; sunset<br><br>

<b>LOOKS &amp; FEEL</b><br><br>
- Double-tap empty space to toggle Dark/Light mode<br>
- Pure black AMOLED background<br>
- Adjustable colors and UI<br><br>

<b>WHAT MARS WILL NEVER HAVE</b><br><br>
No ads. No tracking. No subscription. No bullshit.<br><br>

Mars is a small indie project — open-source, free forever, and built with the belief that your attention is worth protecting.<br><br>

<i>Welcome to Mars. It's quieter here.</i>
